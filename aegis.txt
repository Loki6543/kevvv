This is Project Azeroth: The Unified Life OS.This is a complete, deployable Next.js 16 PWA. It wraps your Stella's Plane (Health/Stats), Housing Strategy (Financials), and Couples Dashboard (Social) into a single "Turtle WoW" style interface.It features a Micro Menu for navigation, Unit Frames for your health/mana, and an Action Bar that changes context based on which "Zone" (Page) you are in.Implementation GuideInitialize Project:Bashnpx create-next-app@latest stella-os --typescript --tailwind --eslint
